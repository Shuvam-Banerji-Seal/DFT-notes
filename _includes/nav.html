{%- comment -%} Top navigation — cream canvas, 64px tall, sticky to the top of every page. Renders the spike-mark + wordmark on the left, the primary menu in the middle, and a "GitHub" text-link + a coral "Read the notes" CTA on the right. Collapses to a hamburger at < 768px. Adapted from the Claude.com design spec (design.md): - `top-nav` background = canvas (#faf9f5) - menu type = nav-link (Inter 14px / 500) - primary CTA = button-primary (coral) - wordmark prefix = spike-mark On mobile the menu becomes a full-screen cream sheet (#mobile-menu) with the same links stacked vertically and a primary CTA at the bottom. The toggle behaviour lives in `assets/js/nav.js`. {%- endcomment -%}
