# 99 — References & further reading

A curated, opinionated reading list. Grouped from "must read" to "deep dive".

## Foundational papers

- **Hohenberg, P. & Kohn, W.** (1964). *Inhomogeneous electron gas.* Phys. Rev. **136**, B864.
  The HK theorems — start here.
- **Kohn, W. & Sham, L. J.** (1965). *Self-consistent equations including exchange and correlation effects.* Phys. Rev. **140**, A1133.
  The Kohn–Sham equations.
- **Kohn, W.** (1999). *Nobel Lecture: Electronic structure of matter — wave functions and density functionals.* Rev. Mod. Phys. **71**, 1253.
  Beautiful historical overview by one of the founders.
- **Levy, M.** (1979). *Universal variational functionals of electron densities, first-order density matrices, and natural spin-orbitals and solution of the v-representability problem.* Proc. Natl. Acad. Sci. USA **76**, 6062.
  The constrained-search formulation of HK.
- **Perdew, J. P. & Schmidt, K.** (2001). *Jacob's ladder of density functional approximations for the exchange-correlation energy.* AIP Conf. Proc. **577**, 1.
  The Jacob's ladder picture.

## Textbooks

| Book | Coverage | Level |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Parr, R. G. & Yang, W.** — *Density-Functional Theory of Atoms and Molecules* (Oxford, 1989) | Mathematical foundations, formal theory | Graduate |
| **Dreizler, R. M. & Gross, E. K. U.** — *Density Functional Theory* (Springer, 1990) | Rigorous, formal, advanced | Graduate / research |
| **Koch, W. & Holthausen, M. C.** — *A Chemist's Guide to Density Functional Theory* (Wiley-VCH, 2nd ed. 2001) | Practical, chemistry-focused | Graduate, applied |
| **Martin, R. M.** — *Electronic Structure: Basic Theory and Practical Methods* (Cambridge, 2004) | Solid-state, plane waves, DFT in practice | Graduate |
| **Engel, E. & Dreizler, R. M.** — *Density Functional Theory: An Advanced Course* (Springer, 2011) | Theoretical depth, TDDFT, response | Advanced |
| **Burke, K.** — *The ABC of DFT* (free online mini-textbook) | Quick, modern, friendly | Introductory |

## Reviews and surveys

- **Becke, A. D.** (2014). *Perspective: Fifty years of density-functional theory in chemical physics.* J. Chem. Phys. **140**, 18A301.
  Excellent 50-year retrospective.
- **Mardirossian, N. & Head-Gordon, M.** (2017). *Thirty years of density functional theory in computational chemistry.* Mol. Phys. **115**, 2315.
  Comprehensive survey of modern functionals.
- **Burke, K.** (2012). *Perspective on density functional theory.* J. Chem. Phys. **136**, 150901.
  Short and balanced.
- **Perdew, J. P., et al.** (2009). *Density-functional theory for strangeness.* (And many similar reviews of Jacob's-ladder rungs.)

## Specialized

- **Time-dependent DFT:** Marques, M. A. L. et al., eds. — *Time-Dependent Density Functional Theory* (Springer LNP 706, 2006).
- **DFT for solids:** Payne, M. C. et al. (1992). *Iterative minimization techniques for ab initio total-energy calculations.* Rev. Mod. Phys. **64**, 1045.
- **Dispersion corrections:** Grimme, S. et al. (2010, 2011, 2017) — DFT-D3, D4 papers.
- **SCAN:** Sun, J., Ruzsinszky, A. & Perdew, J. P. (2015). *Strongly Constrained and Appropriately Normed Semilocal Density Functional.* Phys. Rev. Lett. **115**, 036402.
- **Hybrid functionals:** Becke, A. D. (1993) — B3LYP; Ernzerhof, M. & Scuseria, G. E. (1999) — PBE0; Heyd, J., Scuseria, G. E. & Ernzerhof, M. (2003) — HSE.
- **DFT+U:** Liechtenstein, A. I., Anisimov, V. I. & Zaanen, J. (1995).
- **GW:** Hedin, L. (1965); Onida, G., Reining, L. & Rubio, A. (2002). *Electronic excitations: density-functional versus many-body Green's-function approaches.* Rev. Mod. Phys. **74**, 601.

## Online resources

- **[Burke's "ABC of DFT"](https://dft.uci.edu/)** — free mini-textbook.
- **[CMSE lectures on DFT](https://www.chem.pku.edu.cn/jianghj/DFT/)** — clean slides.
- **[The Nobel Prize in Chemistry 1998](https://www.nobelprize.org/prizes/chemistry/1998/summary/)** — popular-level introduction.
- **[DFT in a nutshell](https://www.psi.ch/theory/dft-in-a-nutshell)** — a clear overview from the Paul Scherrer Institute.
- **Hands-on codes:** VASP (commercial), Quantum ESPRESSO (free, plane waves), SIESTA (free, localized orbitals), CP2K (free, Gaussian + plane waves), ORCA (free for academics), Gaussian (commercial), Psi4 (free).
